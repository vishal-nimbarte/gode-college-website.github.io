About Us

      Dr.Rajendra Gode Institute Of Technology & Research, Amravati is one of the premier institutions of Maharashtra which established in 2008 in the aegis of Late Honorable Dr.Rajendraji Gode. Our institute is known for its excellence to provide higher Education in various fields considering the need of the region of Vidharbha. It is located on sprawling 20 acres of campus on road 6 km form Sant Gadgebaba Amravati University, Amravati.
